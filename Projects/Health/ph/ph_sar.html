Physical Health: Sleep and Rest

Why is sleep so important?

Sleep is an essential function that allows your body and mind to recharge, leaving you refreshed and alert when you wake up. Healthy sleep also helps the body remain healthy and stave off diseases. Without enough sleep, the brain cannot function properly.

How is getting enough sleep and rest beneficial for your body?

When you don't get enough sleep, your immune system is weaker, making you more susceptible to illnesses. Energy levels after healthy sleep are higher, and your mental awareness is more acute. Good sleep is also tied to improved athletic performances, including greater speed, agility and reflexes.

Sleep plays a vital role in good health and well-being throughout your life. Getting enough quality sleep at the right times can help protect your mental health, physical health, quality of life, and safety. The way you feel while you're awake depends in part on what happens while you're sleeping.

What are the benefits of getting a full night's sleep?

  • Sleep can boost your immune system.
  • Getting sleep can help prevent weight gain.
  • Sleep can strengthen your heart.
  • Better sleep = Better mood.
  • Sleeping can increase productivity.
  • Lack of sleep can be dangerous.
  • Sleep can increase exercise performance.
  • Sleep improves memory.

What can lack of sleep do to you?

Some of the most serious potential problems associated with chronic sleep depravation are high blood pressure, diabetes, heart attack, heart failure or stroke. Other potential problems include obesity, depression, impairment in immunity and lower sex drive. Chronic sleep depravationcan even affect your appearance.
