Physical Health: Importance

How will good physical health benefit me?

A person who has good physical health is likely to have bodily functions and processes working at their peak. This is not only due to an absensce of disease; Regular exercise, balanced nutrition and adequate rest all contribute to good health.

How do I improve my physical health?

Regular physical health can make children and adolescents improve cardiorespiratory fitness, build strong bones and muscles, control weight, reduce symptoms of anxiety and depression, and reduce the risk of developing health conditions such as heart diseases. Physical activity keeps the body strong strong and healthy and can improve mental health by decreasing symptoms of depression, anxiety, pain and loneliness. Physical activity can also improve focus, school performance, sleep and energy levels.

In what other ways does physical activity help me?

Physical activity or exercise can improve your health and reduce the risk of developing several diseases like type 2 diabetes, cancer and cardiovascular diseases. Physical activity and exercise can have immediate and long-term health benefits. Most importantly, activity can improve your quality of life.
